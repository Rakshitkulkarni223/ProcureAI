Core Engine

6 Procurement Strategies

StrategyOptimizes ForBest When
BalancedAll factors equally weightedGeneral-purpose procurement
Lowest CostTotal procurement costBudget is the primary constraint
Lowest RiskComposite risk scoreBuying critical or high-value items
Fastest DeliveryMinimum delivery daysUrgent, time-sensitive purchases
Highest ReliabilitySupplier consistency & ratingRepeat orders, reliability matters
Best Long-Term ValueQuality + consistency + warrantyLong-term supplier relationships

The engine dynamically reranks suppliers without changing underlying data — the same catalog produces different "best" suppliers depending on business priorities.
